Reflection

I chose to edit this picture darker than usual because I wanted to change the emotional tone of the image. The stairway already has a slightly creepy feeling — it leads upward into an unknown area, and you can’t see what’s at the top. I tried to emphasize that sense of mystery and uncertainty. Lowering the brightness and deepening the shadows makes the space feel more enclosed and unsettling. Darkness often represents the unknown, fear, or tension, so the edit helps the viewer feel slightly uncomfortable or curious about what might be beyond the visible space. Also cropping it was easy because it was the only one that would look good for the 5’-7’ requirement. I also wanted to show how editing choices can completely transform meaning. A brighter version of the same image might feel safe or normal, but the darker version creates suspense and emotional depth.
